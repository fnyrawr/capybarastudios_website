About our Game Iron Slug

We implemented a First-Person 3D-Shooter game in Unity. It includes some singleplayer missions where players have to fight their way through different maps against AI enemies and turrets. It also includes a multiplayer game mode.
The game should be about having a fun time playing alone or against your friends. You can also submit your scores ingame and view the Leaderboard on our page.

Gamemodes and Maps

Our Game features Singleplayer aswell as Multiplayer modes. In Singleplayer you fight your way through the map against bots fulfilling an objective. There is a leaderboard where you might end up seeing your score if you prove to be good enough. In Multiplayer you can play online with your friends.

  • personSingleplayer Space

    Fight your way through space. Enter the space base, fight your way through the graveyard and get back to earth ASAP.

  • personSingleplayer Arctic

    Fight your way through the mountains and through the city. Try to get away from this lost place.

  • groupMultiplayer Warehouse

    Play online with your friends in this chaotic but interesting looking warehouse.

  • groupMultiplayer Arcade

    Play online with your friends and try to not fall off the platform.

Game mechanics

The following slides introduce the keybindings. You can learn how to move in the game.
Click indicators or slide or swipe to continue.
You may also remap controls for your convenience.

Basic movement

Use WASD to move around. W to move forward. S to move backward. A to go left. D to go right.
Hold Shift to sprint.

Jumping, Crouching, Sliding

Use Space to jump. Crouching can be done with holding Control. You may not wake enemies when crouching around them. Slide with holding Shift and Control while moving.

Aim and Shoot

When holding a weapon you can use the right mouse button to aim a target and shoot with the left mouse button.
You may need to reload. You can do this by pressing the R key.

Interaction with objects

Use E to interact with objects. You can open doors, pick up weapons and more.
Switch weapons with Num Keys.
